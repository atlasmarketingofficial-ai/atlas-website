# Atlas Acquisition Agency

Local SEO and AI Search Funnels for Service Businesses.

Atlas builds inbound funnels for service businesses: top of Google, recommended by AI tools like ChatGPT, and tracked calls every month.

## The Problem

Most service businesses are invisible where customers actually look. It's usually not a quality problem—it's a visibility problem caused by:

- **Google can't tell what you do or where** — Most service business websites have no pages for their actual services or cities.
- **AI gives out a short list. You are not on it.** — When someone asks ChatGPT or Gemini who to hire, it recommends 2-3 companies. If the AI doesn't know you, you don't exist.
- **Bigger budgets are buying your market** — Franchises and private equity backed chains pay agencies to dominate local results.

## Our Services

### Local SEO
Get found for what you do. We build the service and city pages your website is missing, so Google connects your company to the work you actually do and the areas you actually serve.

### Google Business Profile
Win the map pack. We optimize your profile, tighten your service area, build your review count, and keep the listing active so Google keeps showing you.

### AI Search Visibility
Get recommended by ChatGPT. We structure your online presence so AI tools find you, trust you, and recommend you by name.

## The Funnel

Every service we run plugs into one funnel: **search to call**.

1. **Found** — Your company shows up at the top: rankings, map pack, AI recommendations.
2. **Trusted** — Reviews and an active listing convince them you're the real option.
3. **Called** — They call you directly. Every call is tracked so you know where it came from.
4. **Booked** — You close the job. We report rankings, calls, and results every month.

## How It Works

**Step 1: Audit** — We pull up your Google rankings, map listing, and what AI tools say about you right now.
