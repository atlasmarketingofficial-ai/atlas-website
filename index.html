The problem

Most service businesses are invisible where customers actually look.

It is usually not a quality problem. It is a visibility problem, and it has three common causes.

Google cannot tell what you do or where

Most service business websites have no pages for their actual services or cities. If your site never names the service and the town, Google has no reason to show you for it. Someone else gets the call.

AI gives out a short list. You are not on it.

When someone asks ChatGPT or Gemini who to hire, it recommends two or three companies. There is no page two. If the AI does not know you, you do not exist in that search.

Bigger budgets are buying your market

Franchises and private equity backed chains pay agencies to dominate local results. Owner-operators with better work and better reviews get buried under companies with bigger budgets, not better work.
